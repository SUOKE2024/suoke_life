# Suoke Life User Manual

## Introduction

Welcome to Suoke Life, your personal AI assistant for health and life management. This manual will guide you through the features and functionalities of the application.

## Features

### Home

-   **Chat:** Interact with the AI assistant through text-based conversations.

### Explore

-   **Expert Advice:** Get health advice from experts.

### Life

-   **Life Records:** Record your daily activities and manage your life.

### Profile

-   **User Profile:** View and manage your user profile.
-   **Settings:** Configure application settings.

## Getting Started

1.  **Installation:** Download and install the Suoke Life application from the app store.
2.  **Registration:** Create a new account or log in with your existing credentials.
3.  **Home Screen:** The home screen displays the chat interface.

## Usage

### Chat

1.  Type your message in the text input field.
2.  Tap the send button to send your message.
3.  The AI assistant will respond to your message.

### Explore

1.  Navigate to the Explore tab.
2.  View the list of expert advice.
3.  Tap on an advice item to view the details.

### Life

1.  Navigate to the Life tab.
2.  View the list of life records.
3.  Tap on a record to view the details.

### Profile

1.  Navigate to the Profile tab.
2.  View your user profile information.
3.  Tap the settings icon to configure application settings.

## Settings

1.  **General:** Configure general application settings.
2.  **Notifications:** Manage notification settings.
3.  **Privacy:** Manage privacy settings.

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, please refer to the troubleshooting section in the application or contact our support team.
